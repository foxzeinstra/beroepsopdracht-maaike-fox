History

In a former production studio of 2100 square meters, Nxt Museum provides a unique addition to the flourishing creative district of Amsterdam-Noord. Inspired by the international museum landscape where interactive technology-driven exhibits. To offer visitors a unique and often personalized experience, Nxt Museum wants to be the pioneer in the field of emerging tech, new media art and visitor experience in the Netherlands. Nxt Museum was founded by the young entrepreneur Merel van Helsdingen, exhibits large-scale art installations, These are developed through interdisciplinary collaborations between established and emerging artists, designers, technicians, musicians and scientists. For example, there are several exhibitions in a year with multiple types of art in it, for example, there is currently an exhibition of the post-digital art group Random International viewing in Nxt Museum.
